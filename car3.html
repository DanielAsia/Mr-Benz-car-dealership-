MR BENZ DEALERSHIP

FOUNDER AND CEO

Mr. Benz dealership was founded by Daniel Asia Benz in 1950, starting as a modest garage with just two second-hand cars and a vision. Driven by a deep passion for automobiles and an unwavering commitment to customer service, Daniel transformed his humble beginnings into a trusted name in the industry. With a keen eye for quality and innovation, he built the business from the ground up, focusing on honesty, reliability, and long-term relationships. Over the years, Mr. Benz dealership grew into a cornerstone of the community, known not just for selling cars, but for creating a legacy of trust and excellence in the automotive world.

Vision and Mission

Our vision is to empower the youth of this generation by creating meaningful opportunities for those who lack access to essential life and career skills—particularly in driving and effective communication. Our mission is already in motion: we've established over 30 dealerships across South Africa, providing employment and growth opportunities to more than 120,000 young people nationwide. We are committed to building a future where every young person is equipped with the tools they need to succeed, contribute, and thrive.
